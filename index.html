Martin's Tips

  • It is critical that the tank is filled with the proper amount of water. Check the level regularly, and add water as needed.
  • The tank must be cleaned on a regular basis. Your fish create waste that can be toxix. It may cause health problems and in extreme circustances death.
  • Make sure you remember to check the salinity of water in the tanks, and don't forget to feed them!
